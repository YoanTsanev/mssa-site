Work Package 4:

Creating a prototype of a sensor system for research and analysis of the body's health. Creation of algorithms for analysis and decision-making based on information about the health status of the organism;
Head: Assist. Prof. Ph.D. Krasimir Cheshmedzhiev

Activities:

  1. Creation of a prototype of a sensor system for monitoring the health status of the body. The system will take into account various indicators such as: temperature, pulse, heart rate variability, blood oxygen content, physical state of the individual - rest or movement.
  2. Creation of a database storing the received information from the sensor system with the possibility of searching by various signs.
  3. Creation of algorithms for analysis and decision-making based on the information received from the sensor system.
